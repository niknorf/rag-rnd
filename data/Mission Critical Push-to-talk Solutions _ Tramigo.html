AVAILABLE SOFTWARE OPTIONS

Tramigo Mission Critical Push-To-Talk (MCPTT) solutions have three different software options you can choose from based on your intended use cases and team roles

Web-Admin

User friendly Web-Admin interface allows creating, adding and removing users and groups. Users and groups can be also created by uploading an excel file. All admin-functions are available over SOAP-protocol which allows to integrate PTT-management to company CRM and other business services.

Control Center

The Windows PC MCPTT Control Center gives the possibility to monitor, track devices location, and communicate directly to devices. Audio in/out and text-input can be integrated using standard devices and connections, including wired and Bluetooth. Windows Dispatcher support 1-8 monitors allowing flexible scalability from entry-level single-display system to 8-screen video-wall.

Mobile App Dispatcher

The Mobile App Dispatcher is compatible with Android and designed to allow communication with small groups and control center and track devices location as well. Mobile app dispatcher also allows you to request for a call back from the devices.
